What Is ConnectEd?

Creating moments to teach and learn

ConnectEd is an award-winning app designed specifically to connect educators with learners in real-time. In under a minute clinicians can broadcast learning opportunities to interested students, solving the communication problems that arise in busy clinical settings.

ConnectEd for Educators

In less than a minute, share a learning opportunity in real-time.

Much of the daily clinical practice of health professionals working in a hospital or health care setting is an opportunity for a student practitioner to observe and learn.
